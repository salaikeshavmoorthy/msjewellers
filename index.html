About Us

MS Jewellers has been serving Pudukkottai for over 10 years with exquisite gold, silver, diamond, and custom jewellery. Certified with BIS Hallmark, we ensure trust, quality, and elegance in every piece.

Our shop is located on East 2nd Street, Avathar Complex, 1st Floor, No7, Pudukkottai, Tamil Nadu. We are dedicated to providing a royal and modern shopping experience for all occasions.

Visit us to explore our bridal collections, exclusive rings, necklaces, earrings, bangles, and custom designs crafted with perfection.
